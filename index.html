STAGES OF WEBSITE

DEVELOPMENT

STAGE 1

BRIEFING

At this stage, there is an acquaintance with the customer and his project, we discuss what tasks need to be solved. Next, we work out the structure of the site, we determine the terms and prices.

STAGE 2

ANALYTICS

Analyzing user needs, market trends, and competitor websites to inform design decisions.

STAGE 3

PROTOTYPING

Creating interactive wireframes and prototypes to test functionality and user flows.

STAGE 4

DESIGN

Developing the visual design, including color schemes, typography, and UI elements.

STAGE 5

ADAPTIVE

Ensuring the design works seamlessly across all devices and screen sizes.

STAGE 6

THE FINAL

Final review, testing, and delivery of the completed project to the client.
